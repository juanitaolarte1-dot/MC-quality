ABOUT US

Where discipline meets horse-first training

We follow a minimalist equestrian line inspired by EQUUS: clear structure, calm communication, and long-term rider-horse trust.

Our History

MC QUALITY SHOWJUMPERS started as a focused high-performance team for clients seeking consistency in technique and competition readiness.

Today, we support active riders with a structured methodology that combines measurable goals, weekly progression and transparent planning.

Our Philosophy

Vitality, responsibility, horse connection and discipline are the core of each session. We prioritize welfare, clear communication and sustainable progress.

Every rider receives level-based guidance, and each horse is trained within balanced physical and emotional workload.
